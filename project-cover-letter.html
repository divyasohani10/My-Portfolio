Project 08 2026

AI Upwork Cover Letter
Generator

n8n OpenAI Slack Google Sheets LangChain
Overview

The Objective

This project eliminates manual proposal writing for Upwork job applications. When a job post is shared in a dedicated Slack channel, the AI agent automatically retrieves relevant past projects from Google Sheets, matches them to the job requirements, and generates a tailored, policy-compliant cover letter — delivered back to Slack within seconds. No manual writing, no missed proposals.

The Challenge

Policy Compliance Without Losing Quality

Upwork strictly restricts certain words and external contact references in proposals. The AI needed to produce genuinely high-quality, personalized cover letters while following these rules precisely — replacing platform names like WhatsApp and Slack with compliant alternatives, never fabricating project details, only referencing real data from Google Sheets, and keeping length between 150-250 words with a confident, professional tone.

The Solution

Technical Strategy

A Slack trigger listens for new messages in the upwork-cover channel. The job post text is passed to a LangChain AI Agent powered by GPT-4.1-mini. The agent is equipped with a Google Sheets tool (Get Projects) that fetches all past project data including descriptions, tech stacks, results, and links. The AI identifies relevant matches, structures the cover letter according to a defined template (greeting, introduction, job understanding, relevant experience, approach, CTA, closing), applies all compliance rules, and posts the finished letter back to the same Slack channel — ready to copy and submit.

Key Results

Impact

  • Cover letter generated and returned to Slack in under 30 seconds
  • Real project data from Google Sheets used — zero fabrication
  • Full Upwork policy compliance built into the system prompt
  • Consistent quality across every proposal regardless of job type
