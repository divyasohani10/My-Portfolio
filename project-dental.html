Project 09 2025

AI Dental Appointment
Booking System

Retell AI n8n WhatsApp Email OpenAI
Overview

The Objective

This project automates 100% of dental clinic appointment handling. An AI voice agent named Aria answers incoming calls, collects patient details, books time slots, and instantly sends WhatsApp and email confirmations — with zero human involvement.

The Challenge

Complexity at Scale

The key challenge was reliably extracting structured data (name, date, time, phone) from messy, real-world voice conversations and routing it correctly through the automation pipeline, including handling AM/PM ambiguity and varied speech patterns.

The Solution

Technical Strategy

Retell AI handles the voice layer with a warm, empathetic agent named Aria. After the call, a webhook fires to n8n which extracts appointment data using AI summarization, formats timestamps, and triggers parallel WhatsApp (Meta Cloud API) and Email confirmations in real-time.

Key Results

Impact

  • 100% automated call handling
  • 0 missed appointments after deployment
  • WhatsApp + Email confirmation in under 30 seconds
