Overview

The Objective

This project transforms a standard Gmail inbox into an intelligent CRM-aware sales assistant. The system monitors incoming emails, uses AI to detect genuine sales leads, fetches full CRM context from HubSpot including deal stage and call history, generates professional reply drafts, and delivers a daily analytics report — all automatically. Sales teams review and send; everything else is handled.

The Challenge

CRM Context at the Right Moment

The core challenge was making the AI reply feel genuinely informed rather than generic. This required fetching the right HubSpot data (contact details, deal stage, previous notes, call history) at the exact moment of reply generation, and passing it all as context to the AI — while ensuring new contacts were only created for validated leads and never for spam or noise.

The Solution

Technical Strategy

n8n monitors unread Gmail continuously. When a new email arrives, an AI validation step classifies it as a genuine lead or noise. For valid leads, HubSpot is queried for existing contact data including deals, notes, and call history. If no contact exists, one is created automatically. All this context is passed to GPT-4.1-mini which generates a tailored, professional draft saved directly in Gmail — never auto-sent. A second scheduled workflow runs daily, calculates performance metrics (emails received, follow-ups done and missed, average response time, pending drafts), and emails a structured summary report to the team.

Key Results

Impact

  • Zero missed sales leads from inbox
  • AI reply drafts ready in under 60 seconds
  • Automatic HubSpot contact creation for new leads
  • Daily performance report delivered to team automatically
