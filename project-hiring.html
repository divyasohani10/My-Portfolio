Overview

The Objective

This system automates the resume screening and candidate evaluation pipeline for hiring teams. Resumes flow in, an AI evaluator scores each candidate against job requirements, structured scores and summaries are written to Google Sheets, and only the most qualified candidates are surfaced for human review. What previously took days of manual screening is reduced to minutes.

The Challenge

Consistent Evaluation at Scale

Manual resume screening is inconsistent — different reviewers apply different standards, leading to missed talent or wasted time. The challenge was building an AI evaluator that applies the same scoring criteria to every candidate, extracts structured fields reliably from unstructured resume formats, and outputs results in a format that is immediately actionable for hiring managers.

The Solution

Technical Strategy

n8n orchestrates the full pipeline. Incoming candidate data is passed to GPT-4o which evaluates each resume against a defined rubric — scoring skills match, experience relevance, and overall fit as a percentage. The AI returns structured JSON output which n8n writes directly into Google Sheets with candidate name, score, summary, and recommendation. Firestore stores job configuration and evaluation criteria per role, allowing the system to support multiple open positions simultaneously with different scoring weights.

Key Results

Impact

  • Resume screening time reduced from days to minutes
  • Consistent scoring criteria applied to every candidate
  • Structured output written directly to Google Sheets
  • Multi-role support via Firestore job configuration
